  3. Purpose of the Fee: Covers profile screening, interview scheduling, placement facilitation, documentation, and onboarding support.
  4. Mandatory Compliance: Payment is compulsory. Non-payment may result in job cancellation and termination of assistance.
  5. No Hidden Charges: Only the one-time service charge applies; no other fees are involved.
  6. CV Submission at Interview: Candidates must bring a hard copy of their updated CV to interviews.
  7. Job Guarantee Disclaimer: Placement depends on interview performance; registration alone does not guarantee a job.
  8. Background Verification: Cooperation is required; false information may lead to disqualification.
  9. Communication Policy: Updates will be sent only via official WhatsApp or email.
  10. Post-Placement Support: Limited support is provided in early employment stages for joining delays or documentation issues.
